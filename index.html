WHY THE NEED FOR A PEDIATRIC DENTIST?

Dental Treatments require patients to keep their mouth open for long periods of time. Children have various kinds of fear regarding injections,tooth removal in their mind and their narrow intellectual and emotional quotient makes it even more worse. So many a times children do not realize the meaning of simple things. So a special kind of skillset is required in the dentist treating them who can channelize his treatment according to the mental situation in which the child is. These are things which make paediatric dentistry a specialized field under the broad umbrella of Dentistry. During their 9 years of exclusive practice of paediatric dentistry,Dr.Ankush and Dr.Madhumati came up with multiple ways by which they could engage children and treat them smoothly. Example:Bravery Certificate.
